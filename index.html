What Is This?

Thrash Kan Kidz is a card-collecting game built for the thrash & death metal community. 486 parody cards across 6 themed series, with 4 collectible variants per character — Stormy, Decayed, Camouflage, and Vintage. Open packs, trade duplicates, chase the variants you're missing, and complete the headbangers' hall of fame.

No energy bars. No PvP grind. No loot-box gambling. Just collecting, the way it was meant to be.
