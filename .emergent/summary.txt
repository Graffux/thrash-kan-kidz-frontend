The user wants to build a mobile card collecting app for their Thrash Kan Kidz cards.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Core Gameplay**: Users log in, receive coins, and can purchase cards. A collection view shows all cards, indicating which are owned and which are missing.
- **Card Tiers & Unlocks**:
    - **Common Cards**: Purchasable from the shop.
    - **Rare Cards**: Become purchasable after collecting a certain number of cards (10, 20, 30, 40).
    - **Epic Cards**: Become purchasable after achieving consecutive login streaks (7-day, 14-day).
    - **Milestone Cards**: Users receive one free, random, unowned common card every time their collection size grows by 10 cards.
    - **Coming Soon Cards**: A set of cards that are visible in the shop but marked as unavailable for purchase. Some are unlocked via engagement-based milestones.
- **Engagement Milestones**: Specific Coming Soon cards are unlocked by meeting certain criteria:
    - **Dedicated Fan**: 30-day login streak unlocks Maxi Pad.
    - **Big Spender**: Spend 750 total coins unlocks Musty Dave.
    - **Monthly Master**: Log in 20 days in a single month unlocks Chum Araya.
- **Card Interaction**: Cards in the collection should flip over to show the back when swiped.
- **UI/UX**:
    - Custom logo and background on all screens.
    - A celebration pop-up (Modal) when a special card is unlocked/awarded.
    - A user profile/dashboard screen for stats and logging out.
    - Tab navigation with icons.
- **App Store Preparation**: The app should be configured with an icon, splash screen, and necessary metadata.

**User's preferred language**: English
